The Classes We Offer

Jiu-Jitsu

jiu-jitsu is a self-defence martial art and combat sport based on grappling, ground fighting, and submission holds.

Jiu Jitsu is a difficult but rewarding martial art. Unlike many other martial arts it has no striking, instead mostly taking place on the ground and utilising chokes, strangles and joint locks.

Start Learning

Judo

Judo is an unarmed modern Japanese martial art, Olympic sport, and the most prominent form of jacket wrestling competed internationally

Judo is also good for self-defense purposes, preparing people for unexpected circumstances, with the stamina and skill to act in defense of oneself or others.

Start Learning

Karate

Karate is a Japanese martial art whose physical aspects seek the development of defensive and counterattacking body movements.

Karate practice strengthens the mind, develops composure, a clearer thought process, deeper insight into one's mental capabilities.

Start Learning

Muay Thai

Muay Thai, sometimes referred to as Thai boxing, is a combat sport that uses stand-up striking along with various clinching techniques.

Muay Thai boosts your hip mobility Moving your hips regularly through exercise and sports activities while you can.

Start Learning
